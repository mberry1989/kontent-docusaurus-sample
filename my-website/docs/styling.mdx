By default, the CSS Styles for the site can be changed in *my-website\src\css\custom.css *and logos can be changed in *my-website\static\img . *Paths for favicons ,logos, and themes can also be modified in the *docusaurus.config.js *file in the root of the site. More information about styling in can be seen in the [Docusaurus documentation](https://v2.docusaurus.io/docs/styling-layout "Docusaurus styling and layout").
